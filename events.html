Expert Talk Series - 2

    Founder

    Tamil Robotics Club
  • Date: 25th January, 2022
  • Description: On January 25th , from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM, the Robotics Club of Sathyabama hosted an Expert Talk on “Robotics research and its Future”. The event started with the welcome address and by giving the introduction to the speaker by the technical team. Mr. Balaji Thiru Founder of Village Tech School and who is a part of the Tamil Robotics Club started his talk on the topic of Robotics research and its Future. As well the Q&A sessions happened. The meeting came to and end by thanking the speaking and with the formal vote of thanks.

Expert Talk Series - 1

  • Resource: Mr. Prakadesshwaran M
  • Date: 10th October, 2021
  • Description: On October 10, 2021, the Robotics Club at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology hosted a webinar titled "Expert Talk Series - 1." Mr. Prakadesshwaran M, Robotics perception engineer at Drishti, spoke about his career in robotics and his time as an intern at the DRDO's Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (CAIR). The sessions were attended by 105 people who had enrolled. Participants learned how to work with Autonomous Mobile Robots using the Robotic Operating System (ROS) and simulators like Gazebo.
