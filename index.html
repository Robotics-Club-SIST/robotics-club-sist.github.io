About Us

Robotics Club Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is a community of students who derive pleasure from creating mechanical peers that may even be potent to work without human intervention. We provide members mechanical, production, electronic informatory aid besides providing the guidance.

We are a student community, that is aimed at bringing together robotics nerds, exchange knowledge and to promote community driven projects to solve real world problems using robotics and automation.
