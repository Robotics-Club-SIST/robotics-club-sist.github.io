About Us

Robotics Club Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is a community of students who derive pleasure from creating mechanical peers that may even be potent to work without human intervention. We provide members mechanical, production, electronic informatory aid besides providing the guidance.

We are a student community, that is aimed at bringing together robotics nerds, exchange knowledge and to promote community driven projects to solve real world problems using robotics and automation.

WOW - Wonderful Opportunistic Workshop

The Robotics Club is back for another enthralling, fascinating, and enjoyable hands-on workshop that will undoubtedly provide you with a clear understanding of the development boards. The hands-on workshop that we will work on together will have interesting topics such as Arduino, Node MCU, and a few sensors, as well as their updated applications. We will also be briefing you on the Internet of Things (IoT). In this workshop, the participants will be segregated into groups and perform the tasks that we assign based on the principles of the project. The winners will get exciting prizes. We will have fun events too. Do not miss this exciting opportunity because there are limited seats of only 32 available… First come, first served, grab your seats fast and get ready for the ride with hands-on experience on these Arduino boards.
